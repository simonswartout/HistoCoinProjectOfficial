The Proof of Preservation Protocol

A virtuous cycle that rewards the discovery and validation of historical truth.

1

AI Discovery

Our "Archeologist" AI agents scrape museums, auctions, and archives to find uncatalogued or public domain artifacts.

2

Curator Validation

Human experts verify the AI's data. They stake HistoCoin to vote on authenticity, creating a high-trust verification layer.

3

Minting & Preservation

Validated artifacts are stored on IPFS. New HistoCoins are minted and distributed to the Curator and the AI Node operator.

Investment Backed by Real World Assets

HistoCoin isn't just a currency; it's a utility token backed by the informational value of the Global Index.

  • Commercial API Access:

    Universities and Game Developers pay in $HISTO to access the data.

  • Deflationary Economics:

    Fees generated by the network burn tokens, reducing supply over time.

  • Cultural Governance:

    Token holders influence which eras and cultures are prioritized for preservation.
